Hi! I'm Mira Nalbandian, a freelance journalist and fact checker based in Cambridge, MA.

I am currently a senior at Harvard College, graduating in May 2026. I have written for The Progressive Magazine, The Harvard Crimson, and The Harvard Political Review.

I have written about national and international politics, reported on Harvard initiatives, and done data journalism on youth political opinions.

I have also fact checked and researched a variety of articles and academic projects. I am currently co-authoring an academic article comparing Southern African high court decisions with Supreme Court decisions regarding LGBTQ+ rights. I have previously researched for a book with Professor Sandra Susan Smith at the Harvard Kennedy School. Also, I just completed a 30,000-word thesis for my A.B. degree at Harvard College.
